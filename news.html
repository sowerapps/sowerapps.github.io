News

April 12, 2021

The first release of Sower Applications. I invited Harry Plantinga to become a part of this project, and he accepted which is good. Sower Applications are designed to work with ThML files, that was in fact the first design decision. Harry is the 'father' of ThML, as well as the founder of the Christian Classics Ethereal Library which has created the ThML files from which Sower modules are built.

There is currently no release for MAC, so that will be the next priority.
